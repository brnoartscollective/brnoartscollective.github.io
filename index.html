Brno Arts
Collective Values

Art is the expression of the inner experience, bringing life and flesh into the brittle skeleton of existence. In our community, we attempt to approach art intentionally with the reverence of the beauty and importance it holds in the society. Our goal as craftsmen and artists is to constantly immerse and educate ourselves concerning the field we proclaim to partake in. It is paramount for any artist to constantly be learning and evolving with the craft they work in as well as other artistic forms which often carry elements which pervade into all realms of the arts. We consider an important element of this belief is to read and glean from those who came before, paying homage to those who have paved the way for us to be where we are now. As an artist, it is paramount to align ourselves with the concept of Tradition derived from the shoulders of the giants on whom we are meant to stand.
